Work with us

Hairway to Heaven is looking for new people to join us in our journey to revolutionize the hairdressing market in Norway. Below you will find a list of available positions, and a downloadable form you can fill in and fax to us. Common for all our employees is that they are highly skilled in what they do, positive and always ready to give 100%! If you think you are a fit for any of the positions, please apply!


Hair Technician

  • Education: MSc or PhD in hair sciences
  • Work Experience: Should have some prior saloon experience
  • Avalable Locations: Oslo, Trondheim, Bergen and Stavanger

Hair Dresser

  • Education: At least 3 years in hairdressing school
  • Work Experience: Should have worked in high end saloons before
  • Avalable Locations: Oslo, Trondheim and Stavanger

Cleaning Personell

  • Education: A bachelors degree in cleaning is preferred, but not necessary
  • Work Experience: If no degree the applicant should have some prior work experience
  • Avalable Locations: Oslo, Trondheim, Bergen and Stavanger
  • A special attention to detail is important!

Accountant

  • Education: Bachelors degree in accounting
  • Work Experience: None required
  • Avalable Locations: Trondheim, Bergen and Stavanger
